THE DISTINCTION

Two kinds of loss.
Only one of them should be permanent.

Trading Loss

The ordinary cost of the market

Markets move against you sometimes. That's not failure, that's just what participating in a market means. A losing trade is data. It teaches you something and you continue.

Permanent Loss

The ending nobody warned you about

This is the one that removes you from the markets entirely. Your capital is gone, your account is closed, and there's no eleventh trade. Take Profit exists to make sure this never happens quietly.

HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One protected account.

01

Connect your account

Link your existing broker account, or set up a new one with one of our partner brokers. Your capital stays exactly where it already is.

02

Get matched with a manager

An execution specialist is assigned to trade your account under strict, software enforced risk limits, so no single decision can wipe you out.

03

Trade with a floor beneath you

If a severe drawdown ever hits, the platform steps in to restore your account so you can keep participating instead of starting over from nothing.
