THE DISTINCTION

Two kinds of loss.
Only one of them should be permanent.

Trading Loss

The ordinary cost of the market

Markets move against you sometimes. That's not failure, that's just what participating in a market means. A losing trade is data. It teaches you something and you continue.

Permanent Loss

The ending nobody warned you about

This is the one that removes you from the markets entirely. Your capital is gone, your account is closed, and there's no eleventh trade. Take Profit exists to make sure this never happens quietly.
